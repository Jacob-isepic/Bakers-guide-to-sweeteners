🍬 Monk Fruit Guide

Understanding Monk Fruit

✔ Monk fruit is 150–250× sweeter than sugar (pure form)
✔ Most baking uses BLENDS, not pure extract

Common ratios:
• Blend: 1g → replaces ~2g sugar
• Pure extract: mg-level use only

Why blends exist:
Pure extract is too strong to measure safely in normal cooking.
Best usage:
✔ Cakes
✔ Muffins
✔ General baking

Pro tip:
Blends behave like sugar because they include bulking agents.
