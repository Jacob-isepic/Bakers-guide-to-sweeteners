🍬 Monk Fruit Guide

How to make a monk fruit blend:

✔ Step 1: Pick a bulk sweetener
→ Erythritol or allulose (most common)

✔ Step 2: Add pure monk fruit extract
→ ~0.2g to 1g extract per 100g bulk sweetener

✔ Step 3: Mix thoroughly
→ This becomes your usable baking blend

If using pure extract:

⚠ Do NOT bake with it directly
✔ Always dilute into bulk sweetener first

Example ratio:
1g extract → 200–300g sweetener base
